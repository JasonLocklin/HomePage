Custom Search

I have found that searching for technical questions has become rather difficult as ai blog mills have started filling search results with useless garbage. I suggest anyone struggling with this create their own custom "programmable search engine" that searches only sites they consider good sources of information on the topics they need to search. This can be done with Google here.

In my own version, I have various R related community pages and blogs, plus sites like reddit and stackexchange, as well as the roots of mailing list reflectors that have their archives indexed.

Click here for a dedicated page, or use the search below to search only a few tech sites with known human contributors.
